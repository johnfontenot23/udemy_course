About Me

The ultimate generalist product manager/leader who takes a long-term, holistic view of the business and market while I GSD. If you're looking for a highly specialized, domain expert with 'x' years of experience in 'x' industry, I'm not your guy. However, I love research and talking to customers, so I ramp incredibly fast. I've been successfully industry-agnostic in my PM career having worked in and created impact across several industries including B2B SaaS, HRTech, FinTech, HealthTech, ERP, and Marketplaces. I've gone upmarket to Enterprise SaaS with an emphasis on SLG, and I've gone downmarket into SMB with a focus on PLG. I've taken SMB and Enterprise products zero-to-one, and I've helped establish growth loops to drive Acquisition and Retention for mature products.

If you're looking for someone with truly diverse experience, not only is my Product Management background broad, but throughout my career, I've done strategic partnerships, sales, UX Research, UX Design, Product Marketing, and have dedicated myself to the craft of Product. Having worked in startups, I've worn every hat you can name, and I can successfully juggle a lot while context-switching often.

Outside of full-time work, I'm the podcast host of Lessons in Product Management, and I've built several full-stack web apps using Bubble.io (No Code), including Path2Product where I've helped train over 1,200 aspiring product managers who use Path2Product to gain knowledge AND experience that they can showcase in their portfolio and resume. I'm passionate about coaching and teaching, and Path2Product provides that opportunity at scale to help shape the next generation of Product talent.
